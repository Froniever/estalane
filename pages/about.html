ABOUT US

Our Mission

A Rain Angel Resort, our mission is to provide a sanctuary of natural beauty and unparalleled hospitality, where guests can escape the stresses of everyday life and reconnect with themselves, each other, and the world around them.

History

Established in 2018, Rain Angel Resort stands as a testament to the timeless allure of natural beauty and hospitality. Nestled along the pristine coastline of a secluded bay, the resort's journey began with a vision to create a sanctuary where guests could escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life and reconnect with nature in ultimate comfort. Over the years,Rain Angel has evolved and expanded, adding new amenities and experiences while remaining steadfast in its commitment to sustainability and environmental stewardship. Solar panels now dot the rooftops, providing clean energy to power the resort, while organic gardens supply fresh ingredients to the onsite restaurants. The surrounding wilderness has been designated as a protected nature reserve, ensuring that future generations can continue to marvel at its beauty for years to come. Today, Rain Angel stands as a beacon of tranquility and a testament to the enduring power of nature. Whether seeking a romantic getaway, a family adventure, or simply a moment of respite from the chaos of modern life, guests from around the world continue to flock to this idyllic retreat, where the rhythm of the ocean and the whisper of the wind offer solace to weary souls. As it enters its fifth decade, Rain Angel remains dedicated to its founding principles, inviting guests to escape, unwind, and reconnect with the wonders of the natural world.
